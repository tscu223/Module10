Kentucky Cumberland River and Hiking Paths


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Teya Cuellar

This map shows the eastern Kentucky portion of the great Cumberland River. All hiking trails along or near the river are also shown. My hope was that the map could help visualize ways of getting to Lake Cumberland or Cumberland River as well as where to go if one also wanted to hike. This map was made using QGIS 2.18.25. Data retrieved from OCS and UGSG NHD.
